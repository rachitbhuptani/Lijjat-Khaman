16:00The story so far

One recipe.
Now two doorsteps.

One khaman recipe, three decades undefeated.

What began at Kankaria in 1995 with one steel steamer and a handwritten recipe is now a small Ahmedabad institution. The recipe hasn't changed. The welcome hasn't either. Only the map got bigger.
